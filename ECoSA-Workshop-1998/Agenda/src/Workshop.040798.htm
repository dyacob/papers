Objectives of the Workshop

  • To identify the list of languages that use Ethiopic characters
  • To identify organizations that are to be consulted for Ethiopic character set determination
  • To discuss and produce a consensus on technical problems like rarely used characters, punctuation marks, numerals, and sorting orders
  • To identify interested members on the issue so as to establish the technical committees
