\noi
\begin{tabular}{lll}

$\backslash$2) Indemn : aderu?  & $\backslash$  Good Morning.&(Lit: How did you spend the night?) $\backslash$ \\
$\backslash$3) amesegnalehu::   & $\backslash$  Thank You. $\backslash$ & \\
